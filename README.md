# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis (MySQL)

## 📌 Objective
To analyze customer churn behavior and identify key factors affecting customer retention in a telecom company using SQL.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- MySQL
- Excel (Data Cleaning)
- (Optional) Power BI

---

## 📂 Dataset
Telco Customer Churn Dataset (Kaggle)

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed
- Churn Rate Calculation
- Customer Segmentation (Contract, Payment Method)
- Revenue Loss Analysis
- Tenure-Based Lifecycle Analysis
- Multi-Factor Churn Analysis

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Month-to-month contract customers have the highest churn rate
- Customers with high monthly charges are more likely to churn
- Lack of tech support significantly increases churn
- Most customers churn within the first year

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Offer discounts for long-term contracts to reduce churn
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers
- Provide better customer support services
- Introduce flexible pricing plans for high-value customers

---

## 📈 Conclusion
Data-driven retention strategies focusing on pricing, service quality, and customer lifecycle management can significantly reduce churn and improve revenue.
